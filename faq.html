Getting started

Creating the business on Sync

Now that you’ve downloaded the Sync business app, let’s get you started. Firstly, click on Your logo here, create a business card. The guided onboarding takes you through setting up a business name, modules you want in your business app, name of your first catalog, and admin name and phone number details.

Remember that you can change all of this once your business is set up too.

Setting up your business profile

Now that you have a business on Sync, let’s learn how to set up your business profile. Firstly, click on the business name inside the home-screen, on top-left and then click on the wheel on the extreme right - the settings icon. This is your business profile.

You add a logo, change the business name, phone number and many other details. There are few important sections here: 

Catalog

Creating a catalog

Catalog is the core essence of your business. This is where people browse through products and shop from you. With Sync you can add items, create categories and have images, descriptions and many other features too. 

If you have thousands of products you can bulk upload your list to Sync with pictures, description, price and other advanced features too.

Editing a catalog

With edit catalog, you can make changes to your existing catalog and also unlock advanced features like minimum order, miscellaneous charges, enabling pre-payments and more.

Adding and managing catalogs

Your business can have multiple catalogs on Sync. This is especially useful if you have products which are totally different from each other and you want to collect orders separately for both catalogs. For example, there might be a group of products which do not have any minimum order and one group might have a minimum order. In this case, you can create two catalogs.

Remember that customers will not be able to add items across catalogs in one order. They will have to place different orders for different catalogs. 

Order Management

Sync enables your business to have complete control over the ordering process. This ensures that you do not need to do multiple followups and have a clear view of the orders.

View summary

If you spend too much time creating excel sheets and collate all orders, this is a real time-saver for you. The summary gives you a snapshot of the entire orders that have been placed on the catalog. You can sort them as per address or by date. You can even download excel, pdf reports, as well as manage summaries with filters like paid, unpaid, confirmed, unconfirmed and other order statuses.

Request orders

Have a new catalog which you want to push to your members? Request order sends out a phone notification to all your members and brings them to the new catalog you have just uploaded.

Send order confirmations

After a customer has placed their order, you can use this to confirm the same. Order confirmations are important if you have turned on, confirm before pay option, where the customer will see payment option only after your confirmation. 

Send delivery confirmations

Notify your customers after the delivery is done through this feature. This sends out a phone notification confirming deliveries and also completes the order cycle for the customer.

Send payment reminders

In case there are customers who have placed an order and not yet paid, this option allows you to send them reminders via phone notifications to pay for the same.

Inviting Customers

Now that you’ve learnt how to operate your app, it's time to invite your customers. You can invite your customers in three ways, as of now:

Share invite code

Every business has a 6 digit invite code. Your customers who are downloading Sync can go to I HAVE AN INVITE CODE and enter the same. Your invite code is mentioned on the top right of the app under the words SHARE.

Share invite link

Click on the SHARE button on the top right of your business app and you can choose to share your invite link over WhatsApp, Telegram, and other installed apps.

Clicking on this link will take customers to the app store or play store depending on the device and after the app is installed, it will take them to your store automatically, without having to key the invite code.

Tip: Keep this invite link to share it with big groups, pages and communities.

Invite contacts

You can send an SMS burst to any number of people in your contact list too for free.

  • Click on your business name on the top left
  • Click on the invite contacts icon
