Business Profile

  1. Click on your business name on the top left hand corner inside your app
  2. Click the edit (pencil) icon next to number of members

Your Business

  • Add a photo
  • Click on + and give access to images
  • Select your business logo
  • Tip: Upload a picture in the ratio of 1:2, width:height for best results

Business name
This is what is visible to your users. You can change your business name if you want to.

Mobile number
This is the number you entered while signing-up. This is the number people will reach out to when they have queries.

GST Number
Enter your 15 digit GSTIN. This is not mandatory. This is only if you want to showcase your taxation number in PDF order summary.

Welcome Message
Set a message to welcome your members when they join your app. They will see this the moment they come on your app for the first time.

Success Message
Use this message to communicate with customers right after they’ve placed an order. For eg: Thank you for placing your order, we hope to serve you again. Your order number is @order_number
Tip: @order_number generates a random order number for you to track each orders.

Pickup Address
If you have enabled pickup in your fulfilment options, then this is the address where your customers will pick up from.

Privacy

Hide Customer Address
Click on this to ensure that the full address is not visible to other members of your group.
Why is this here? In a lot of apartment use-cases, businesses prefer to showcase addresses since it is only within the community.

Tip: If you run a business that is not specific to any apartment, you should hide customer address

Deliveries

Enable Delivery
If this is enabled, the user will have to enter their address at checkout once.

Enable Pickup
If this is enabled, the user will see your business address where they need to pick up goods from.

Payments

UPI handle or payment url
Enter the UPI handle where you want to receive payments to. You can use any UPI handle here. Alternatively, in case you use other payment provider, you can add their payment links here.

UPI is free and has the benefit of instant transfer.

Pricing for other methods like Paytm for business or Instamojo is between you and the provider, at the time of sign-up.

Important: When you activate this method, the customer journey is as follows

  1. Customer adds items to cart and swipes to place order
  2. Customer comes to ORDERS section and clicks on MAKE PAYMENT
  3. This opens up the available UPI apps through which they make payment
  4. They click on MARK PAID and confirm payment

UPI transactions happen outside the app and hence customers need to manually confirm payment.

Razorpay
This module was built for businesses that are scaling and need a fool-proof reconciliation system. For this you need to:

  • First, create an account with ​Razorpay and complete KYC
  • Once the documents are reviewed and approved Navigate to settings ​→ ​API Keys​ ​→ ​ Generate Key
  • Here you will see two keys
    • Razorpay Key id
    • Secret Key (be sure to save this since it shows up only once and need to be regenerated again if you haven’t noted it)
  • Enter Razorpay key and Razorpay secret, and you’re ready to integrate seamless payments for your business

CATALOG

Add catalog
Click on ADD or SELECT CATALOG
Click on + ADD CATALOG

Catalog Name
Enter a name that suits the list of items. For eg: Fruit & Grocery, Takeaway Menu.

Catalog Description
Describe your catalog. What should people expect out of this? Are there any important things like minimum order or estimated time of delivery that you want to share?

Click on ADD CATALOG and it gets created.

Add items
Click on + Add an item
Items have the following fields:

  • Click on ​+​ to add a picture
  • Mention the ​Item name​. Tip: Add the qty for clarity
  • Mention the ​item price
  • Category name​ under which this item belongs.
    If you leave this blank product will be added without category
And you’ve added your first product! Congratulations.

Edit catalog item
Long press on the item you just added and it opens up Edit catalog item. Here you can
Edit the details you provided during ​Add an item.
Add advanced features to your product like: Description for your item that customers can read about under ​Item description.

Pricing

Final Price
This is what the customer pays when they add the items to cart and place an order

Pre-discount price
This is the slashed price where people see the original price. This helps customers visualise the savings you provide.

Scheduling
This will let your customers pick a preferred time for fulfillment

Sorting
Want an item to be on top? Just put the serial number as 1. This can help you showcase your most important products first. By default the sorting of items will be done in the order you added them.

CATEGORIES
Edit category
Long press any category to Edit the same.
You can edit the ​Category name
You can change the order of category by mentioning ​Serial number like item sorting. The default sorting of a category is A-Z.
Enable ​Expanded​ category if you want people to see items in your category as soon as they land. The default category is set to closed which means that customers will click on the category to expand.

Locking
Locking blocks customers from placing further orders. This can be done with an ​item-level lock ​which you can enforce in situations like out-of-stock and cut-off time.

Catalog-level lock ​blocks users from placing orders on the entire catalog.

Manage catalogs
Add a new catalog or use your existing catalog.

Publish catalog
NOT LIVE means that your customers cannot see this catalog. Click on Publish catalog and confirm to make it LIVE.

EDIT CATALOG
Click on the menu icon before your catalog name and click on Edit catalog. You will find advanced features to ensure you have the perfect catalog.

Info

Payments
Confirm before pay
If you click this, this will allow your customers to pay only after you confirm their orders. If this is off, customers will get an option to pay you the moment they place an order, if payments are ON for the catalog.

INVOICE
Footnote
Add a message that you want to include in your PDF downloads for your customers. This could include basic terms and conditions or any custom message.

CHARGES
Fixed charges per order
This is where you can add your delivery fees if it is applicable for your customers. This will be presented to the customer at the time of the checkout.

Minimum order value
Fix the amount at or above which you will accept orders. If the order is less than minimum order value, customers can not place their orders.

Miscellaneous

ADD your own items
This will allow your customers to create a free list of items that are not in the catalog. The availability and payment for these items will have to be done offline. This is especially useful if you have a long list of items

HIDE locked items
This will hide locked items from the catalog. You may want to use this when you have a significant number of items in your catalog locked. It will help customers see only what is available and what you’re accepting orders for.

ALLOW cancellations
This will let customers cancel unconfirmed and unpaid orders.

SHOW orders count
This will show the orders count below every catalog item. This helps customers find social proof. Please note that order count is hidden if you have item descriptions.

Preview Catalog
Click here to view your catalog as a member of your business. Another important feature of Preview catalog is that you can place an order on behalf of your members too.

Bulk Upload Items
If you have lots of items then Bulk upload is your friend.
First, Download .xlsx template. This template has 6 column names.
Name, Price, Category name, Description, Image url, Pre-discount price

Note: Image url needs to be a publicly accessible link.

Once you’ve put all the items as per the format above click on PICK A FILE and upload the file. Confirm the column name in Pick mappings and click on Send.

Sit back and relax as Sync does the heavy lifting. Get tens of thousands of items listed in minutes with the super-easy bulk upload items feature.

Duplicate catalog
Create a duplicate of your catalog with this option. This is different from selecting a previous catalog from Manage catalogs as this resets all the orders and starts fresh.

Orders

View Summary
This gives you a list of all your orders. You can change this view from date to address too, where it will sort it from A to Z.

An individual order consists of 3 sections
General details: Customer name, phone number, PDF, Call
Order details: Item name, quantity, total and fulfillment preference.
Fulfillment status: Ordered, confirmed, paid, delivered and date of order + order number

The PDF next to the individual order can be used as an invoice or a record to be sent to the customer. The call icon connects you to the customer incase of any queries.

You can download the summary as a PDF or as an excel sheet by customers, apartment, order or summary.

Filter
You can filter your order summary by Payment, Confirmations, and Delivery status. Filtering can also be done via dates.
You can have multi-level filters too. For eg: Those who have paid and confirmed but not yet delivered in between selected dates.
