Payments

UPI handle or payment URL
Enter any UPI handle where you want to receive payments. UPI is free and has the benefit of instant transfer.
Important: When you activate this method, the customer journey is as follows

  1. Customer adds items to cart and swipes to place order
  2. Customer comes to ORDERS section and clicks on MAKE PAYMENT
  3. This opens up the available UPI apps through which they make payment
  4. They click on MARK PAID and confirm payment

UPI transactions happen outside the app and hence customers need to manually confirm payment.

Locks
Locking blocks customers from placing further orders. This can be done in situations like out-of-stock and cut-off time.

There are item-level and catalog-level locks.

Search

The super-fast search will help your customers to find what they are looking for. The search takes into account, the category name and the item name.

NOT LIVE means that your customers cannot see this catalog. Click on publish catalog and confirm to make it LIVE.

By default, the first catalog is in a published mode. Every additional catalog needs to be turned LIVE from here.

Charges

Minimum order value
Fix the amount at or above which you will accept orders. If the order is less than the minimum order value the differential amount that needs to be added will be indicated to proceed with the checkout.

Misc charges per order
This is where you can add any extra fees at a catalog level. This will be presented to the customer at the time of the checkout. For eg: Flat delivery fee, convenience fee.

Miscellaneous

Show orders count
This will show the number of orders placed below every catalog item. This helps customers know what is popular. Please note that order count is hidden if you have item descriptions.

Hide locked items
Help customers see only what is available to order. You may want to use this when you have a significant number of items in your catalog locked.

Add your own items
This will allow your customers to create a free list of items that are not in the catalog. The availability and payment for these items will have to be done offline.

Allow file uploads
This will allow customers to upload files before or after placing an order. Even business can upload file against a customer order.

This is used by pharmacies for checking prescriptions. Another use is by businesses in customized printing of mobile covers, cakes. Also used by customers or business to upload the payment screenshots.

Allow cancellations
This will let customers cancel unconfirmed and unpaid orders.

Currently, a business or customer cannot see cancelled orders int the dashboard. We're working on it.

Confirm before pay
Use this option if you want to take payment only if the order is confirmed by you. If this is off, customers will get an option to pay you the moment they place an order. For this to work, you should have Enabled payments for the catalog.

Request Prepayment
If you want to incorporate e-commerce like experience where you want to collect payments at the time of placing an order, use this feature.

Preview catalog
Click here to view your catalog as a member of your business. Another important feature of the preview catalog is that you can place an order on behalf of your members too.

Bulk upload items
If you have lots of items then Bulk upload is your friend.

First, Download .xlsx template. This template has column names like

Name, Price, Category name, Description, Image URL, Pre-discount price, tax, packaging charges

Note: Image URL needs to be a publicly accessible link.

Once you're ready click on pick a file and upload the file. Confirm the column name in pick mappings and click on send .

Sit back and relax as Sync does the heavy lifting. Get tens of thousands of items listed in minutes with the super-easy bulk upload items feature.

Duplicate catalog

Orders

Take control over the entire order journey of your customers!

View summary
This gives you a list of all your orders. You can change this view from date to address too, where it will sort it from A to Z. Refer to order summary in business settings on how to change it.

An individual order consists of 3 sections:

General details: Customer name, phone number, PDF, Call

Order details: Item name, quantity, total and fulfilment preference.

Fulfilment status: Ordered, confirmed, paid, delivered and date of order + order number

Order PDF
The PDF next to the individual order can be used as an invoice or a record to be sent to the customer.

Call icon
The call icon connects you to the customer in case of any queries. For customers, this icon is linked with the business number.

Download summary
You can download the summary as a PDF or as an excel sheet by customers, apartment, order or summary.

Filter
This is a superpower to manage your business! You can filter your order summary by payment, confirmations, and delivery status . Filtering can also be done via dates.

You can have multi-level filters too. For eg: Those who have paid and confirmed but not yet delivered in between selected dates.

Request orders
This is a super-feature for your business app. Send out a phone notification to your customers announcing your catalog and asking them to order. You will love this!

You can either select all members at once or select them one by one too.

Send order confirmations
Send out notifications confirming the order that customers placed. Customers love it! This is also necessary if you have activated confirm before pay

After sending order confirmations, customers won't be able to cancel orders if you've allowed it in the catalog settings.

Send delivery confirmations
After you've delivered, use this feature to notify them that the delivery is done. Creates a closure to the order.
